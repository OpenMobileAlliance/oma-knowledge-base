## LwM2M TestFest (April 20–23)

A structured, hands-on testing environment running continuously across all four days.

:::ShSegment
---
ui:
  wrapper: bg-oma-cyan-400 dark:bg-oma-cyan-900 dark:text-stone-500 rounded-xl p-2

  ### TestFest Focus
  - End‑to‑end interoperability testing between independent LwM2M implementations
  - Conformance checks against current LwM2M specifications
  - Capturing issues, feature requests, and improvement feedback from live test systems
