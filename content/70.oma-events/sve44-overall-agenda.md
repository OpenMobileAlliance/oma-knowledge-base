## Interop \& Conformance Lab (April 20–23)

A structured environment for hands-on testing across real implementations. 

:::ShSegment
---
ui:
  wrapper: bg-oma-cyan-900 text-stone-500 rounded-xl p-2

  - End‑to‑end interoperability testing between independent solutions
  - Conformance checks against current specifications
  - Capturing issues, feature ideas, and improvement requests from live systems
