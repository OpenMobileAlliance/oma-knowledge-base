# OMA LwM2M Test Event & WG Members' Meetings

The Open Mobile Alliance (OMA) regularly organizes LwM2M Test Events — known as Specification Validation Events (SVEs) — to ensure the interoperability and compliance of LwM2M implementations across the global IoT ecosystem. These events bring together engineers and implementers from OMA member and non-member companies alike, providing a confidential, multi-vendor testing environment where Client and Server implementations are validated against OMA standards.  
  
Beyond testing, each SVE includes OMA Working Group Meetings where members collaborate on the evolution of LwM2M and related specifications — making these events a unique combination of hands-on technical validation and strategic standards development. Whether you are looking to verify your implementation, benchmark against other vendors, or contribute to the future of IoT standards, OMA Test Events are the place to be.

**Stay tuned for the announcement of the next OMA Test Event.**
