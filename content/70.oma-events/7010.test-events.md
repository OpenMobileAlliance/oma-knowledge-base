## Event Overview

#### Two Connected Parts, One OMA Event

- **Test Event**: Technical engineers and implementers validated interoperability and conformance in a structured Specification Validation Event (SVE).
- **OMA WG Meetings**: OMA members collaborated on work items, roadmaps, and technical discussions related to LwM2M and other specifications.

## Part 1 - OMA LwM2M Test Event (SVE-44)

#### Hands-on interoperability and conformance testing for LwM2M and Smart City solutions.

### Practical Details

* Dates: **20–23 April 2026**.
* Location / Format: **Kraków (Poland) / Onsite & Virtual**.
* Host: **AVSystem**

> SVE-44 Test Event has concluded. Results will be posted here when available.

## Part 2 - OMA Working Group Meetings

#### Collaboration on the evolution of OMA standards and work programs.

### Practical Details

* Dates: 21–23 April 2026.
* Location / Format: Kraków (Poland) / Onsite.
* Participation: OMA members and non-members.
* Plenary session (April 22, 2026): Seven presentations delivered under a single emerging narrative — LwM2M has reached its inflection point, from building standards to deploying them at scale.

_A special thank you to all Plenary session presenters and participants for their valuable contributions and engagement in shaping the future of LwM2M_.

> OMA WG Meetings have concluded. Thank you to all who participated.
