### Usage ShSocial
#### Presentation
This is the display format for the `ShSocial` constructor, designed to showcase Social Media posts.
##### X (Twitter) embeded post

::ShSocial
---
src: "1757162766115176926"
---
::

This is how it is constructed:

```md
::ShSocial
---
src: "1757162766115176926"
---
::
```
